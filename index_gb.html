Where?

Apartment Marija is located on the island of Rab, at Banjol 159. It is modernly decorated, air-conditioned and has satellite TV. The apartment is suitable for 3 + 2 persons. Has a secured parking for the vehicle. Within 100 meters there are sandy and rocky beaches and many different facilities. It is located near the city center and various restaurants, shops and shopping centers.

Island Rab

The island of Rab is situated on the northern Adriatic Sea as part of the Kvarner archipelago. With the city of Rab, the island is inhabited by 7 more places: Lopar, Barbat, Banjol, Palit, Kampor, Mundanije and Supetarska Draga. Rab has a population of about 9,000 inhabitants. The people are working in organized tourism for more than 125 years.
